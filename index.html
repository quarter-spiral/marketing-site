Mission Kontrol

More players for your game.

Mission Kontrol is the DIY publishing service for Online, Social and Mobile game developers.

No haggling, no compromise on your vision. Just a powerful set of tools to find the players who will love your game.

Mission Kontrol helps spread your game across the internet and put your game in more players’ hands.

No up front costs and you keep 70% of any money we make together.

With Mission Kontrol you can easily publish your game in less than a day. But this is only a hint of how you can enhance your game. Learn more...

Publish your game to any or all of our supported venues. Without any coding. Within hours. For free.
