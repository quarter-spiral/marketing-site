Show me the Money

We offer the works to monetize your game. Everything is a la carte and highly customisable so you can put your game and your players first!

Microtransations

The best way to make money on your games is to sell directly to your players. We support selling Virtual Goods (a.k.a Microtransactions).

Avertising

Use our flexible toolset to integrate any or all of the following:

  • Pre Roll Ads
  • Interstitial Ads
  • Light Box Ads
  • Game Frame Ads

Subscriptions

We offers you an easy way for the player to sign up for and manage his subscription.

We also make it easy to offer the player discounts for buying packages of months or years in advance.

Donation Support

Empower you players! Automatically remove ads for any player who supports your game with a donation.

Try before you buy

Allow your players unlimited access to your game for a trial period, then restrict access to some or all of the game unless he purchases the game in full.

For those developers who feel like free to play games “nickel and dime” the player with microtransactions, the try before you buy model of the shareware days is the best option.
