About Quarter Spiral

About Quarter Spiral

In its 40+ year history, the games industry has been in constant flux. In recent years, almost every aspect of making games has changed alongside an explosion of platforms, practices and players. But publishing remains largely unfazed.

Quarter Spiral was founded on a simple idea. Publishing should be a service that puts developers and players before profit. Publishing should serve independence and innovation. Publishing should put you in control of your destiny.
