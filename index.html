Quarter Spiral

Our mission is to change game publishing

In its 40+ year history, the games industry has been in constant flux. In recent years, almost every aspect of making games has changed alongside an explosion of platforms, practices and players. But game publishing remains largely unfazed.

Quarter Spiral was founded in 2012 with a simple idea. Publishing should be a service that puts developers and players before profit. Publishing should serve independence and innovation. Publishing should put you in control of your destiny.
