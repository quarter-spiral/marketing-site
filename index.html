Mission Kontrol

More players for your game

Mission Kontrol by Quarter Spiral is the DIY publishing service for Online, Social and Mobile games. Mission Kontrol will help you find more players and make more money.

No negotiation, no compromise on your vision. Just a powerful set of tools to connect you to the players who will love your game.

Quarter Spiral

Our mission is to change game publishing

Frustrated by the outdated patterns of video game publishing Quarter Spiral was founded in 2012. As industry verterans we previously worked hard to fix the system from within, but true disruption and innovation works best when unemcumbered.
