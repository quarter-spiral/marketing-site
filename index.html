No longer available

The Quarter Spiral services & sites have been discontinued as off 9/9/2013.

What happened?

When we launched the Kickstarter campaign for Enhanced Wars, our company had been self-funded for well over a year. We needed a successful campaign in order to have the resources to turn the Enhanced Wars demo into a fully featured game that meets our quality standards. After the first two weeks of the campaign, it was clear that we were not going to meet our goal and decided to shut down the campaign and company.

We are especially grateful for the support of our backers and for the small, dedicated community of players that enjoyed the early demo.

We are no longer in a position where we can continue to work on this game full time and see no path to continue working as Quarter Spiral and finish Enhanced Wars.

Thank you all for your support. It was a great ride while it lasted.

Thorben, Ethan & Alex
